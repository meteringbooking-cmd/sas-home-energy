Our Mission

Energy Expertise You Can Rely On

At SAS Home Energy, we are committed to delivering energy assessments of the highest standard. Our team of fully qualified Domestic Energy Assessors combines rigorous technical knowledge with a thorough understanding of the UK's diverse housing stock — from new builds to centuries-old cottages.

"We believe every property owner deserves an accurate, honest assessment — one that genuinely reflects their home's energy performance and opens the door to meaningful improvements."

We understand that navigating energy regulations can feel complex. That's why we take the time to explain every aspect of your certificate, discuss what the ratings mean for you, and offer practical guidance on how to improve your property's energy efficiency where it matters most.

Whether you're a homeowner preparing for a sale, a landlord ensuring compliance, or a developer completing a new build, you can count on SAS Home Energy to deliver a professional, prompt, and dependable service every time.
